I am fascinated by people: our thoughts, actions, and feelings. We are incredibly complex, and many times I wish I could borrow someone else's mind and see what it's like to view the world from a different perspective. What interests me most is that we are all unknowingly influenced and controlled by subconscious thoughts and messages in the world. The challenge is to figure out what works for a single person, and then generalizing that to a greater audience. This is the problem I want to work on and hope to solve in my projects. I aim to understand people so I can create beautiful and intuitive designs for them.
