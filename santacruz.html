Mobile Museum Exhibit

User Research, Prototyping, Model Building

This Spring 2014, I am working on a project with the Santa Cruz Children's Museum of Discovery. My team and I are working on creating a mobile museum exhibit for children between the ages of 5 and 8 years old. As of now, we've decided to go along with creating an exhibit focusing on music and sound .

This page will be a work in progress as we complete each step in the human-centered design process. Coming up: User Research and User Needs!
